# Complex Numbers

1. **What is the sum of $(3 + 2i) + (4 - 5i)$?**  
   - A. $7 - 3i$  
   - B. $1 + 7i$  
   - C. $7 + 3i$  
   - D. $7 - 7i$
   - E. None of the above

2. **Multiply $(2 + 3i)(1 - 4i)$ and find the result.**
   - A. $14 + 5i$  
   - B. $14 - 5i$  
   - C. $-10 + 5i$  
   - D. $-10 - 5i$
   - E. None of the above

   - E. None of the above
