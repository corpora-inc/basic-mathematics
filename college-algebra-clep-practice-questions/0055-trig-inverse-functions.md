# Trig Inverse Functions

1. **What is the exact value of $\sin^{-1}(-\frac{1}{2})$?**
   - A. $\frac{\pi}{6}$
   - B. $-\frac{\pi}{6}$
   - C. $\frac{5\pi}{6}$
   - D. $-\frac{5\pi}{6}$

2. **Find the exact value of $\cos^{-1}(0)$.**
   - A. $\frac{\pi}{4}$
   - B. $\frac{\pi}{2}$
   - C. $\pi$
   - D. $\frac{3\pi}{2}$

3. **Determine $\tan^{-1}(1)$.**
   - A. $\frac{\pi}{4}$
   - B. $\frac{\pi}{3}$
   - C. $\frac{\pi}{6}$
   - D. $\pi$

4. **What is the value of $\sin^{-1}(\sin(\frac{3\pi}{4}))$?**
   - A. $\frac{\pi}{4}$
   - B. $\frac{\pi}{3}$
   - C. $\frac{3\pi}{4}$
   - D. $\frac{\pi}{2}$

5. **Evaluate $\cos^{-1}(\cos(\frac{5\pi}{3}))$.**
   - A. $\frac{\pi}{3}$
   - B. $\frac{2\pi}{3}$
   - C. $\frac{5\pi}{3}$
   - D. $\frac{\pi}{6}$

6. **If $\theta = \tan^{-1}(\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}})$, find $\sin(\theta)$.**
   - A. $\frac{1}{2}$
   - B. $\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}$
   - C. $\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}$
   - D. $\frac{\sqrt{3}}{3}$

7. **What is $\sin(\cos^{-1}(\frac{2}{5}))$?**
   - A. $\sqrt{\frac{21}{25}}$
   - B. $\frac{3}{5}$
   - C. $\frac{\sqrt{21}}{5}$
   - D. $\frac{\sqrt{21}}{25}$

8. **Find $\tan(\sin^{-1}(\frac{3}{5}))$.**
   - A. $\frac{4}{3}$
   - B. $\frac{3}{4}$
   - C. $\frac{\sqrt{16}}{5}$
   - D. $\frac{\sqrt{16}}{3}$
