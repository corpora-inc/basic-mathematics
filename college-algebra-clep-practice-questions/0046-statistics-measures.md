# Statistics Measures

1. **Which measure of central tendency is most affected by extremely high or low values?**
   - A. Mean
   - B. Median
   - C. Mode
   - D. Range

   - A. 6.4
   - B. 6.5
   - C. 6.6
   - D. 7.0

3. **Consider the numbers: 2, 3, 3, 7, 10. Find the median.**
   - A. 3
   - B. 4.5

   - D. There are outliers on both ends.
