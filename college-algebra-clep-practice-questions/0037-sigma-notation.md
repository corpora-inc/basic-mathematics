# Sigma Notation

1. **Evaluate the sum: $$\sum_{i=1}^{5} i^2$$**  
   - A. 15  
   - B. 30  
   - C. 55  
   - D. 225

2. **Calculate the sum: $$\sum_{k=3}^{7} (2k + 1)$$**  
   - A. 35  
   - B. 45  
   - C. 55  
   - D. 65

3. **Find the sum of the series: $$\sum_{n=0}^{4} 3^n$$**  
   - A. 30  
   - B. 40  
   - C. 50  
   - D. 60

4. **Evaluate: $$\sum_{m=2}^{6} (m^2 - m)$$**  
   - A. 40  
   - B. 45  
   - C. 50  
   - D. 55

5. **Determine the sum: $$\sum_{p=1}^{4} \frac{1}{p}$$**  
   - A. $\frac{25}{12}$  
   - B. $\frac{15}{12}$  
   - C. $\frac{13}{12}$  
   - D. $\frac{23}{12}$

   - B. 6  
   - C. 7  
   - D. 8

   - B. 50  
   - C. 54  
   - D. 60
